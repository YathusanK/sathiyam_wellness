Sathiyam: Where Hope Lives in Truth and Promise

Support for mental wellness.

Sathiyam Wellness Foundation is a safe, non-judgmental space supporting mental wellness in the Tamil community. We provide guidance, resources, and counselling to youth and families, helping end stigma and empower healing, hope, and resilience.

How We Help

  • Addiction recovery guidance
  • Mental health education
  • Family and caregiver support
  • Community resource navigation

Addiction Support

Provides compassionate care pathways to help individuals overcome addiction, rebuild their lives, and restore hope, confidence, and purpose.

Mental Empowerment

Specialized resources and compassionate support to nurture emotional resilience and promote long-term mental wellness within the Tamil community.

Community Support

Fosters an inclusive network where the Tamil community can find support, strength, and connection on their journey to healing.

Immediate Support & Resources

Your journey to wellness begins with a single, courageous step. If you or a loved one are experiencing a crisis or need urgent support, please reach out to dedicated help channels for immediate guidance and professional care during critical moments.
